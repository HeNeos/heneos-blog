Poisson Equation

−∇⋅(q(u)∇u)=f -\nabla \cdot (q(u)\nabla u) = f−∇⋅(q(u)∇u)=f

Here, the coefficient q(u)q(u)q(u) makes the equation nonlinear.

Membrane Poisson Equation

−T∇2D=pin  Ω={(x,y)∣x2+y2≤R}-T\nabla^{2}D = p \quad \text{in}\; \Omega = \lbrace (x,y) \big\vert x^{2}+y^{2} \leq R \rbrace−T∇2D=pinΩ={(x,y)​x2+y2≤R}
